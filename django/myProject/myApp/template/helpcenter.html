Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: How do I create an account?
    A: Click on the "Register" button on the homepage and fill out the required information.
  • Q: How can I reset my password?
    A: Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password?" to receive a password reset link.
  • Q: What types of music can I upload?
    A: You can upload any original music that you own the rights to, including songs, beats, and instrumentals.
  • Q: How do I contact support?
    A: You can reach our support team by visiting the "Contact Us" page or emailing us at support@youremusicplatform.com.
  • Q: Is there a mobile app available?
    A: Yes, our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play.
